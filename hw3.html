Working with images

These two images both represent the same picture of the Golden Gate Bridge, however the one on the right is optimized, while the one on the left isn't. The tool that I used to optimize the one on the right is calledJPEG Optimizer. This tool compresses images to a lower quality, which is largely unnoticeable but helps reduce download time. Optimizing this picture resulted in a huge improvement. Based off the Developer Tools metrics, the one on the left took 36.89 milliseconds to download, while the one on the right took 21.6 milliseconds to download.
